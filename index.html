About Your Instructor

My name is Max and I'm the instructor at SkyCadet Flight Simulator Center. A former student pilot at the Faculty of Aeronautics, Technical University of Košice (TUKE), Slovakia, with most of my flight hours on the Tomark Viper SD-4. My sessions are safety-first and confidence-building, designed for complete beginners and aviation enthusiasts alike.

  • Developed a strong foundation as a student pilot at TUKE, with significant time on the Viper SD-4.
  • Calm, structured coaching with clear briefings and checklists.
  • Focus on standard procedures, situational awareness, and good airmanship.
  • Sessions tailored to your goals: first take-off/landing, IFR-style approaches, or airline flows.

Training Approach

Expect a concise pre-flight briefing, cockpit tour, and shared objectives. We start simple, use standard callouts, and increase complexity at your pace. After landing we debrief with practical tips and next steps.

  • Pre-Flight Briefing: Quick overview, cockpit orientation, and goal setting.
  • Simulation Start: Begin with simple maneuvers, using standard callouts.
  • Progression: Gradually increase difficulty at your comfort level.
  • Debrief: Review performance, practical tips, and next steps.
  • No Experience Required: Just bring curiosity and a willingness to fly!
