Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our simulators, who can fly, and how to book. If you don't see your question, contact us via the booking form.

No experience required. Sessions are beginner-friendly with a concise briefing, cockpit tour, and clear objectives before your take-off.

- Starter Flight (Viper SD-4): perfect first experience focused on takeoff/landing and basic handling.
- Pilot Experience (Airbus A320 or Boeing 737NG): sightseeing and a taste of airline automation/procedures.
- Captain Pro: full navigation flows (Dublin-Cork, Dublin-London flights) or a challenge scenario.

Durations vary by package and include briefing: approx. 40 minutes total (Starter Flight), 60 minutes total (Pilot Experience), and up to 90 minutes total (Captain Pro). See Packages for details.

We welcome aspiring pilots of all ages. Under-16s should be accompanied by a responsible adult. A guest may observe; some sessions allow dual controls with the instructor.

Yes. You fly a simulator under constant instructor supervision. If you're prone to motion sickness, tell us - we can adjust views, scenarios, and pacing to keep you comfortable.

We'll do our best to help. Let us know any mobility or sensory needs in advance so we can prepare the cockpit and tailor the session.

Comfortable clothing and footwear. If you use glasses, bring them. We provide all checklists and materials you'll need.

If your plans change, contact us as early as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate a new time.
